Support

This page is the public support surface for MusicTube. It is intended to stay visible without login so users and reviewers can understand how to get help before authorizing the app.

How to get help

Use the public project issue tracker for app questions, playback bugs, and account-library syncing problems.

Open GitHub Issues Privacy Policy

Privacy and account concerns

If you no longer want MusicTube connected to your Google account, use Library > Account > Delete MusicTube Data to remove the app's local session, downloads, and on-device activity, then revoke app access from your Google account permissions page if you want to fully disconnect the Google account authorization as well.

Privacy and legal details are published on the linked policy and terms pages.

Common troubleshooting steps

  • Sign out and sign back in if account data or playlists appear stale.
  • Confirm the app is using a valid iOS OAuth client and redirect URI.
  • Reopen CarPlay after the phone app finishes restoring your session.
  • Check your network connection if search or library loading fails.

For reviewers

The public-facing app pages for MusicTube are the home page, privacy policy, and terms page on this same site. These pages describe the app's purpose, data use, and public support path without requiring login.
