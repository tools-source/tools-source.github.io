iPhone and CarPlay

Your YouTube library, shaped for the road.

MusicTube is an independent iPhone and CarPlay app that helps signed-in users browse their own YouTube likes, playlists, uploads, and playback queue in a cleaner in-car and on-phone experience.

What this app does

MusicTube signs in with your Google account so it can read your public profile basics and your read-only YouTube library data, then presents that content in an app layout designed for playback and browsing.

Account access Read-only access to the YouTube library data needed to show playlists, likes, uploads, and search-based music results.
On-device state Session and playback metadata are stored locally on the device to support sign-in persistence, lock screen controls, and CarPlay.
Independent product MusicTube is not affiliated with Google or YouTube and does not operate a separate hosted MusicTube account system.

Library-first browsing

Users can open playlists, liked songs, and uploads pulled from the YouTube account they explicitly sign into.

Playback built for iPhone and CarPlay

MusicTube supports queue navigation, now playing metadata, remote controls, and a matching CarPlay browsing experience.

Transparent data use

The app explains what account information is accessed, why it is requested, and where users can read the privacy policy and terms.

Data and permissions at a glance

MusicTube requests only the information required to authenticate the user and display the music-related account data they ask the app to load.

Google profile basics

Name and email can be used to show who is signed in and support account restore between launches.

YouTube read-only data

Playlists, liked items, uploads, and search results are used to build the Home, Search, Library, and CarPlay screens.

Playback metadata

Now playing details are used for lock screen controls, remote commands, and CarPlay integration while audio is playing.

Local-only storage

Session data and lightweight playback cache stay on the device and are not copied into a MusicTube-operated account backend.
