MusicTube

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026

Summary

This Privacy Policy describes how MusicTube handles information when you use the app on iPhone and CarPlay.

MusicTube is designed to work primarily on your device. The app signs you in with your Google account so it can read your YouTube profile, playlists, liked videos, and related music metadata. MusicTube does not run its own customer account system or store your library on a separate MusicTube backend.

MusicTube is an independent app experience and is not affiliated with Google or YouTube.

Information MusicTube Accesses

  • Google account profile information such as your name and email address, provided during sign-in.
  • YouTube account data that you choose to make available through the app, such as playlists, liked items, uploads, and search results.
  • Playback state and media metadata needed for lock screen controls, CarPlay controls, and Now Playing features.
  • Local app data stored on your device, such as sign-in session data and cached playback information.

How Information Is Used

MusicTube uses this information to:

  • authenticate you with Google;
  • load and display your YouTube music-related content;
  • support playback, queue controls, CarPlay browsing, and media controls;
  • keep you signed in between launches; and
  • improve reliability and troubleshoot app errors on-device.

Storage and Security

  • OAuth session data is stored locally on your device, including secure local storage used by iOS for sensitive credentials.
  • Sensitive session information is intended to stay on-device and is not uploaded to a MusicTube-operated server.
  • Cached metadata may remain on-device until you sign out, remove the app, or clear the device.

Sharing

MusicTube does not sell your personal information.

MusicTube shares data only as needed with the services you directly use through the app, including:

  • Google Sign-In and Google OAuth services
  • YouTube and YouTube Data API services
  • Apple system frameworks that provide audio playback, lock screen controls, and CarPlay integration

Third-Party Services

When you use MusicTube, your use of Google and YouTube services is also subject to Google's and YouTube's own terms and privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can sign out to remove the stored local session from the app.
  • You can use the in-app Delete MusicTube Data action to remove the local session, downloads, likes, recent plays, and recent searches stored by MusicTube on your device.
  • You can revoke the app's Google account access from your Google account settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove its locally stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

MusicTube is not intended for children under 13, and the app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version included with the app and repository should be treated as the current version.

Contact

For privacy questions, visit the public support page or use the project issue tracker at github.com/tools-source/musicTube/issues.
